More possibilities with RBK.money

Payments in 60 countries around the world

RBK.money is licensed by the FCA UK. Accept payments and work with clients in UK and other countries.

Connection within 48 hours

Complete a simple online registration and upload documents, and we will check the website and confirm the connection on the same day, providing access to payment processing.

Next day money withdrawal

Manage the funds received from clients in your Personal Account. Set up reimbursement transfers to your checking account according to a schedule that suits you.

One contract for all payments

Sign one contract to integrate popular payment tools and online cash registers, set up mass payouts, auto payments, and other functions.

Clear payment forms

Your customers can easily make purchases on the website or in the app. Instant data verification and the ability to change the payment method make 97% of payments successful.

Protection against fraudulent payments

RBK.money's anti-fraud solution is based on data analysis collected over 17 years of serving more than 30,000 clients. Our company undergoes an annual audit to ensure compliance with PCI DSS standards.

Industry Solutions

We offer safe and reliable technologies for accepting all types of payments in various business sectors. Our solutions are developed considering the specifics and requirements of each industry.

Online Retail

Online stores can set up the holding of funds on the payer's card until the confirmation of product availability in their warehouse.

Travel

Travel agencies receive convenient automated calculations with tour operators, reducing their operational costs.

Taxi

The use of payment technology with linked bank cards in mobile applications without 3D-Secure reduces the payment time for passengers.

Subscription Services

Accepting recurring payments according to a set schedule helps avoid payment delays, and customers enjoy uninterrupted services.

Gaming

For computer game developers, popular payment tools among gamers are available: mobile commerce, electronic money, and wallets.

Utilities

The best part is the reduced rates for paying utilities, electricity, and gas. Plus, you can set up auto-payments or bill distribution.

MFOs and Banks

For quick repayment of loans and credits with a reduced commission rate, support for the AFT (Card2Account) payment acceptance channel has been implemented.

Marketplaces

Instant payment splitting among different recipients when paying for an order from several suppliers.
